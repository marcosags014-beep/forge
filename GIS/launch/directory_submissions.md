## Description (200 words)
FORGE is a personal life operating system that tracks Health, Body, Wealth, and Mind simultaneously — then uses AI to tell you exactly what to focus on today.

Most people run 4 separate apps that never talk to each other. They spend 30 minutes every morning figuring out what to prioritise. FORGE collapses this into 60 seconds: log your vitals, read what Oracle flagged, close the app, go do the thing.

The core metric is the Alignment Score — how often you keep the promises you make to yourself. Every task you create is a commitment. Past-due and incomplete tasks register as broken promises. Your Alignment Score is the gap between who you say you are and what you actually do.

Oracle (the AI layer) sees all four domains simultaneously: "Your HRV has been declining while your training load peaked — today is recovery. Your cash flow is negative this week — no discretionary spending."

Free tier: full tracking + 10 Oracle queries/day. No account required. All data stays on your device.
Pro: unlimited Oracle + AI Weekly Review (€14.99/mo or €99/yr).

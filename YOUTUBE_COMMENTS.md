Comment: Altini's work is rigorous and underappreciated — he consistently pushes back against oversimplified HRV advice. Someone going through a high-stress week might have tanked HRV even with perfect sleep and nutrition. The data is most valuable when it's connected to the full picture of your life, not looked at in isolation.
